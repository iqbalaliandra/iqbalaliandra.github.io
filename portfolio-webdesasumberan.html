Sumberan Tourism Village Website

The project focuses on promoting Desa Sumberan, a picturesque village located in Candibinangun, Pakem, Sleman, Yogyakarta. The website is designed to showcase the cultural and natural attractions of the village. It includes various sections such as navigation, a welcome header, about the village, services, gallery, testimonials, and contact information.

The "Tentang" section highlights the serene beauty and rich cultural heritage of Desa Sumberan, emphasizing traditional ceremonies and the warmth of the local community. The "Pertunjukan" section details cultural performances like Jatilan, Kethoprak, Karawitan, and Bregada, which are integral to the village's cultural fabric. The "Galeri" showcases images of these cultural events and the natural beauty of the area. The website also features testimonials from visitors and provides contact information for further inquiries. The design integrates responsive elements, ensuring a seamless experience across different devices.
